Info:

This magic sword is The Sword for the Seeker. With this, the seeker can unleash it's full potential. This can only happen when the holder is a true seeker, appointed by a wizard of the First Order. Zedd was the last one to do this by appointing Richard as the new seeker. The Sword of Truth can cut through anything as long as the wielder believes it's evil. When this sword is used to slay something, the magic counteracts itself by showing the wielder why that decision was wrong (gives psychic damage). Anger can weaken this effect.
